Pizza

Description

A flat, open-faced baked pie of Italian origin, consisting of a thin layer of bread dough topped with spiced tomato sauce and cheese.

ingredients

  • 1 Â½ cups shredded part-skim mozzarella cheese
  • 5 cherry tomatoes, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons torn fresh basil leaves
  • 4 small fresh mozzarella balls (bocconcini), thinly sliced

Steps

  1. Heat a 10-inch non-stick skillet over medium-high heat
  2. Arrange tomato slices, basil leaves, and fresh mozzarella slices on the melted cheese
  3. Gently slice pizza onto a cutting board; cool for 1 minute before slicing
